Welcome to Detroit Lite,
Logitech's Circular Design Scorecard

This tool asks 8 product-specific circular design questions, selected as the most important strategies for your project type, to guide and encourage circular design decisions.

Developed by Logitech’s Design for Sustainability team, it simplifies the in-depth Detroit Circular Design Index, based on internal studies, external engagements, and industry best practices.
